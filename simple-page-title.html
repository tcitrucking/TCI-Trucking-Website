company profile

TCI Trucking LLC began with a simple promise: to build a carrier grounded in integrity, faith, and genuine respect for the people we serve. Founded in Coeur d’Alene, Idaho, by two drivers, we understand firsthand what dependable service looks like—and what fair treatment truly means on the road and in business. We are a family-run company, not a corporation, and every decision we make reflects the values we live by. As Christians, we place God at the center of our company, trusting His guidance in every mile we travel and every partnership we form. Our journey officially began in 2025, and while we are a growing carrier, our heart and commitment remain rooted in humility, honesty, and service. At TCI Trucking, you’re never “just another load” or “just another driver.” Whether you’re a broker looking for reliability or an owner-operator seeking a company that truly cares, we are here—always reachable, always accountable, and always grateful for the opportunity to earn your trust. With God’s blessing and the hard work of our team, the road ahead is bright. And we’d be honored to travel it with you.


our mission

At TCI Trucking LLC, our mission is to deliver dependable, honest, and service-driven transportation solutions rooted in faith, family values, and integrity. We strive to treat every broker, shipper, and driver with respect, transparency, and genuine care—building partnerships that last far beyond a single load. We are committed to providing safe, on-time, and top-quality service while creating an environment where owner-operators feel valued, supported, and empowered to succeed. Guided by God, we aim to grow with purpose, serve with humility, and make a positive impact on every mile we drive.
