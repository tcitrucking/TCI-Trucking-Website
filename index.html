WHAT WE OFFER

Tailored Logistic Services

Dispatch Service

At TCI Trucking, we proudly dispatch exclusively within the United States, offering both regional and long-haul options, whichever best fits your goals. We operate coast-to-coast with no forced dispatch, allowing owner-operators full control to choose the loads they want to haul.

✓ Fully transparent load details

✓ 24/7 access to rate confirmations

✓ Honest, flexible, reliable support

Amazon Lease On

Whether you're an owner-operator looking to work with TCI Trucking or simply want to lease onto our company for access to the Amazon Relay load board, we're here to support you. By leasing on, you'll receive full access to view and book your own Amazon loads—no matter where you live.

✓ Access to Amazon Relay load board

✓ Dispatch support when you need it

✓ Independence with company backing

Tire Program

TCI Trucking also offers a valuable tire discount program for our owner-operators, featuring major, trusted tire brands for both trucks and trailers. Whether you're running under our authority or leasing onto the company for Amazon access, you can take advantage of these exclusive savings.

✓ Major trusted tire brands

✓ Discounts for trucks and trailers tires

✓ Reduce operating costs

Fuel Cards

At TCI Trucking, we provide fuel card options through two trusted partners to help our owner-operators maximize savings on the road. One program offers $0.40 per gallon off at any Love's location nationwide, while the second can deliver discounts of up to $0.68 per gallon.

✓ $0.40/gal off at Love's

✓ Up to $0.68/gal savings

✓ Flexible savings nationwide
